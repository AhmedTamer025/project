Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Heat a lightly buttered pan.
  3. Pour a small amount of batter.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with honey or maple syrup.
